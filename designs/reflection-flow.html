What changed from the brief, and why. Ordering is now reflection → skills → ANSAT, with mapping folded into the save screen as auto-suggested, pre-ticked, editable standards rather than a manual mid-flow gate. The three reflective prompts share one screen. New/Renewed is history-derived with a one-tap override. Free text is accepted but queued for normalisation rather than written straight into the corpus. Autosave is shown on every screen. A standalone “just log a skill” path exists so the high-frequency behaviour isn’t trapped behind the low-frequency one.

Still open — your call. (a) Whether the reflection prompts stay fully visible or reveal progressively as each is filled; (b) whether ANSAT 1–5 self-scoring ever enters this flow or stays a facilitator action; (c) the trigger that gets a tired student to open this at all — notification, home nudge, or facilitator prompt — which matters more than any screen here and isn’t designed yet.
